Poem

                        In Rotterdam where rivers meet the sea,
                        The skyline soars with bold integrity.
                        Bridges like fingers touch the sky,
                        And buildings dance where dreams can fly.

                        From the ashes of war, a city rose,
                        With colors bright and form that glows.
                        Cube Houses stand, a playful sight,
                        In modern grace and urban light.

                        The Maas flows gently by the shore,
                        Its rhythm guides the city’s core.
                        In Rotterdam, where visions blend,
                        A future bright, a world to mend.
